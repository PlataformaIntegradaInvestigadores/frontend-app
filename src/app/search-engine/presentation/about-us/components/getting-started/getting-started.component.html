About CENTINELA Project

What is Centinela?

Centinela is a constantly evolving project developed by students from the Faculty of Systems Engineering at the Escuela Politécnica Nacional as part of their final degree project. The primary purpose is to offer a platform that makes information about Ecuador's scientific production more visible through a retrieval system with three types of search and a dashboard section containing graphs of interest related to the country's scientific output.

Additionally, Centinela is designed as a platform to support decision-making and academic interaction. Registered users can share relevant content, form research groups, and participate in a consensus workshop that facilitates the decision-making process to determine the optimal topic for the group to work on. In this way, Centinela aims to be an appealing tool that streamlines the search for scientific information and supports decision-making in the field of research.

What can you find in Centinela?

CENTINELA as a Retrieval System

Find specific Authors, discover experts on a topic through Relevant Authors, and explore how Ecuadorian scientists contribute to a specific area with Relevant Articles.

CENTINELA as an Analytic Tool

Visualize trends to identify emerging research areas and explore demographic data to observe the geographical distribution of scientific production.

CENTINELA as an Academic Interaction Space

Beyond being a search tool, Centinela is a space for the academic community to make posts share content of interest, connect with colleagues, and explore other users' profiles.

CENTINELA as Decision Support

Create teams to participate in a consensus workshop consisting of three phases to determine an optimal topic to work on together. CENTINELA guides the process transparently.
